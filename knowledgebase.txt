## 1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
GuardAI is a high-performance financial security application designed to detect fraudulent transactions in real-time. It utilizes a CatBoost machine learning model specifically tuned for highly imbalanced financial datasets.

## 2. TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
- Model Architecture: CatBoost (Gradient Boosting on Decision Trees)
- Accuracy Metric: 99.9% Recall & 1.00 Precision (State-of-the-Art performance)
- Decision Threshold: 0.9526 (Optimized to eliminate false positives)
- Core Logic: Feature Engineering focusing on balance discrepancies ("Balance Errors").

## 3. DATA INPUT REQUIREMENTS (THE 6 PILLARS)
To analyze a transaction, the agent/user must provide:
1. Transaction Type: (PAYMENT, TRANSFER, CASH_OUT, DEBIT, or CASH_IN).
2. Amount: The total currency value of the transaction.
3. Old Balance Org: Sender's balance before the transaction.
4. New Balance Orig: Sender's balance after the transaction.
5. Old Balance Dest: Recipient's (Receiver) balance before the transaction.
6. New Balance Dest: Recipient's (Receiver) balance after the transaction.

## 4. INTERNAL LOGIC & FEATURE ENGINEERING
The system creates two "Synthetic Features" internally that are the strongest predictors of fraud:
- errorBalanceOrig: (newbalanceOrig + amount) - oldbalanceOrg
- errorBalanceDest: (oldbalanceDest + amount) - newbalanceDest
If these values are not zero, it indicates a mathematical mismatch in the transaction, which is a high-risk fraud indicator.

## 5. USER FAQ FOR AGENT GUIDANCE
Q: What is the 'Receiver'?
A: The Receiver is the destination account getting the funds.

Q: Why are so many balances needed?
A: Fraudsters often try to hide money movement. Checking 'before' and 'after' balances on both sides reveals if money was "created" or "vanished" illegally.

Q: What should I do if a 'Fraud Alert' appears?
A: A Fraud Alert means the transaction matches a known scam pattern. The transaction should be flagged for manual review or immediate suspension.
